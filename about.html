About Us

Excellence in Precision Manufacturing

Spectronic Technology Sdn Bhd is a leading manufacturer of precision engineering solutions, specializing in high-quality stencils and custom jigs for the electronics manufacturing industry. With years of experience and dedication to excellence, we deliver innovative solutions that meet the highest industry standards.
